About DraftMyForms

We believe creating professional documents should take seconds, not hours. DraftMyForms combines AI intelligence with 36,000+ templates to help businesses work smarter.

Our Story

DraftMyForms was built out of a simple frustration: creating professional business documents took too long. Whether it was an invoice for a freelance project, a contract for a new client, or an NDA before a partnership discussion, the process always involved hunting for templates, manually filling fields, and fighting with formatting.

We set out to change that. DraftMyForms is an AI-powered document platform that lets anyone describe what they need and get a polished, professional document in seconds. No wrestling with templates. No formatting headaches. Just describe your document and let the AI handle the rest.

Built by WMK Speciality Services LLC, DraftMyForms serves businesses, freelancers, and professionals across 25+ countries who need reliable, professional documents without the overhead of expensive software or legal teams.
